# Project Vision

The Tricycle Cargo Bike project aims to revolutionize urban transportation by providing an efficient, eco-friendly alternative to traditional delivery methods. Our vision is to create a sustainable, practical, and community-oriented solution that enhances mobility and reduces congestion in city environments.

## Milestones

1. **Research and Development (Q1 2026)**  
   - Conduct surveys to understand community needs.  
   - Analyze existing solutions and identify gaps.

2. **Prototyping (Q2 2026)**  
   - Design and build the first prototype.  
   - Gather user feedback to optimize design.

3. **Testing and Iteration (Q3 2026)**  
   - Perform extensive testing of the prototype.  
   - Refine design based on testing feedback.

4. **Production Planning (Q4 2026)**  
   - Develop a production plan based on final design.  
   - Begin sourcing materials and partners for production.

5. **Launch and Marketing (Q1 2027)**  
   - Launch the Tricycle Cargo Bike to target markets.  
   - Implement marketing strategies to reach potential users and partners.

6. **Community Engagement (Q2 2027)**  
   - Engage with local communities to promote usage.  
   - Gather ongoing feedback for future improvements.
