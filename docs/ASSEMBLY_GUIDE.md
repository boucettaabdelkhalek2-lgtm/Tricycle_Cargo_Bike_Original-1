# Assembly Guide for Tricycle Cargo Bike

## Step-by-Step Assembly Instructions
1. **Unpack all components**: Lay out all parts including the frame, wheels, handlebars, and accessories.
2. **Install the front wheel**: Attach the axle of the front wheel to the front fork and secure it with the provided nuts.
3. **Attach the rear wheels**: Position the rear wheels on the frame's rear axle, ensuring they align properly, and secure.
4. **Mount the handlebars**: Insert the handlebars into the headset and tighten the stem clamp.
5. **Connect the brake system**: Follow the attached instructions to guide the cables through the brake levers and pads.
6. **Install the seat**: Slide the seat post into the frame and adjust its height to your comfort.
7. **Attach the cargo area**: Securely attach the cargo platform ensuring all mounting points are used.
8. **Final checks**: Ensure all parts are secured, and the wheels spin freely.

## Safety Precautions
- Always wear a helmet when riding the tricycle.
- Ensure that the brakes are functioning properly before each use.
- Regularly check tire pressure and the condition of the tires.
- Do not exceed the weight limit specified in the user manual.

## Tools Required
- Allen wrenches (specific sizes will vary)
- Adjustable wrench
- Screwdriver (flat and Phillips)
- Torque wrench (optional but recommended)

## Troubleshooting Tips
- If the tricycle does not steer properly, check that the handlebars are attached securely.
- For brake issues, inspect the cables for frays and ensure the pads are aligned.
- If the wheels wobble, verify that all connections are tight and check for any damaged components.

## Conclusion
Follow this guide carefully for successful assembly and safe operation of your Tricycle Cargo Bike!
