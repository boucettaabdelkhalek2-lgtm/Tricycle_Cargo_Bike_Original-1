# Maintenance Guide

## Introduction
This guide provides instructions for the proper maintenance of the Tricycle Cargo Bike to ensure its longevity and optimal performance.

## Regular Maintenance Checks
1. **Tire Pressure**: Check the tire pressure regularly. Ideally, it should be between 40-50 PSI.
2. **Brake Inspection**: Ensure that brakes are functioning properly. Test them before every ride.
3. **Chain Lubrication**: Lubricate the chain monthly with appropriate lubricant. Make sure to wipe off excess oil.

## Seasonal Maintenance
- **Spring**: Inspect tires for wear and replace if necessary. Check brakes and add lubrication to gears.
- **Summer**: Clean the bike thoroughly to remove dust and debris. Check tire pressure weekly.
- **Autumn**: Prepare the bike for winter by ensuring all components are rust-proofed.
- **Winter**: Store the bike in a dry place and protect it from snow and ice.

## Troubleshooting Common Issues
- **Flat Tire**: Replace or repair the tube or tire as necessary.
- **Squeaky Brakes**: Check brake pads and replace them if worn down.

## Conclusion
Regular maintenance is key to ensuring the longevity and optimal performance of your Tricycle Cargo Bike. Happy riding!
