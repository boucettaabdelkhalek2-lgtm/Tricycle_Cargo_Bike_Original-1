# Safety Guidelines

## Pre-Ride Checks
1. **Inspect the Vehicle**  
   - Check for any physical damage to the frame, wheels, and brakes.
   - Ensure that tires are inflated to the recommended pressure.

2. **Check Brakes**  
   - Test both front and rear brakes to ensure they are functioning properly.
   - Ensure brake pads are not worn out.

3. **Inspect Lights and Reflectors**  
   - Ensure front and rear lights are working (if applicable).
   - Check that all reflectors are clean and properly positioned.

4. **Secure Cargo**  
   - Ensure that cargo is stowed securely and will not shift during the ride.
   - Do not exceed the weight limit specified by the manufacturer.

5. **Wear Safety Gear**  
   - Always wear a helmet.
   - Consider additional protective gear, such as knee and elbow pads.

6. **Weather Check**  
   - Be aware of weather conditions. Avoid riding in extreme weather.

7. **Personal Readiness**  
   - Ensure you are alert and physically fit to ride.
   - Avoid riding under the influence of alcohol or drugs.

8. **Familiarize Yourself with the Route**  
   - Know your route beforehand and look for safe paths or alternatives if needed.  

Stay safe and enjoy your ride!
