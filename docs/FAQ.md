# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## 1. What is the Tricycle Cargo Bike?
The Tricycle Cargo Bike is a versatile bike designed for transporting goods and passengers with ease.

## 2. How much weight can it carry?
The Tricycle Cargo Bike can carry up to 300 kg.

## 3. Is it suitable for all terrains?
Yes, it's designed to handle various terrains, including urban roads and off-road paths.

## 4. What is the battery life?
The average battery life is approximately 40 miles on a full charge.

## 5. Are there any accessories available?
Yes, we offer several accessories, including cargo boxes, lights, and safety gear.

## 6. How do I maintain my bike?
Regular maintenance involves checking brakes, tires, and battery status. Always refer to the user manual for detailed guidance.
