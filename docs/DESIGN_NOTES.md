# DESIGN NOTES

## Design Rationale
This project was designed to create a versatile and efficient cargo bike that meets the needs of urban transport. The goal was to ensure ease of use, durability, and environmental friendliness.

## Technical Specifications
- **Frame Material:** Aluminum alloy for lightweight and strength.
- **Wheel Size:** 26 inches for optimal maneuverability.
- **Cargo Capacity:** Up to 200 kg.
- **Brakes:** Hydraulic disc brakes for reliable stopping power.
- **Gearing:** 7-speed gear system for various terrains.

## Materials
- **Frame:** Aluminum alloy
- **Wheels:** Steel spokes and rubber tires
- **Cargo area:** Durable weather-resistant fabric for protection
- **Brakes:** High performance hydraulic system

## Design Decisions
1. **Material Choice:** Aluminum was chosen for the frame to keep the weight down without sacrificing durability.
2. **Cargo Capacity:** A balance between functionality and weight distribution was prioritized to ensure stability while riding.
3. **Brake System:** The hydraulic brake system was implemented to increase safety, especially with heavy loads.

This file serves as an overview of the critical design elements that guide the construction and use of the Tricycle Cargo Bike.
